that give life to unique characters and creatures.

KHIRA

KHIRA is now launched and ready to meet
the players who enjoy the innovative games.

P2E

P2E is a concept that adds the concept of token economy in F2P (Free To Play)
which most traditional mobile games have adhered to so far,
and mining items that actually have economic value while playing games.

Acquired reward

In our games, users can earn points as rewards while hunting creations or
completing quests through mechanics.
The acquired reward can be swapped into a KHIRA token in the game.

Use the KHIRA token

  • Purchase or sell mechanical NFTs or item NFTs in the NFT store implemented in the game.

  • Transfer to the exchange and
    trade with other crypto assets.

  • Transfer to the
    exchange and cash it.

CREATURE HUNTERS NFT

Mechanics

In games produced by Creature Hunters, you can purchase NFT mechanics
with various abilities or obtain NFTs through various events in the game.
The acquired NFT may produce various performances
in the game according to the assigned ability value.
NFT's unique capabilities reflected in performance
include mobility, strength, and defense.

Creature

With a mechanic, you can execute an AR-based battle.
If you win the battle, you can get the creatures you fought!
When the creatures are strengthened and synthesized
to foster them above a certain level, they can be minted as an NFT.

NFT Market

  • 01

    NFT's value, content, and ownership are stored on a blockchain that activates smart contact and cannot be falsified,
    and proof of ownership can also be transparently and safely disclosed on the public blockchain.

  • 02

    As the NFT of tokens creates the original content and ownership concepts and lays the foundation for value
    recognition, the ecosystem of digital asset transactions, games, and metaverse games can be expanded. In addition,
    various types of NFT attempts can secure many types of consumers.

  • 03

    NFT Market sells NFT-made games and entertainment channels of Creature Hunters' own games and entertainment
    channels, as well as other character trading cards and goods.
    In the future, it will promote an NFT ecosystem that applies O2O through goods.

NFT Ecosystem

The ecosystem of Creature Hunters consists of three main elements.
Creature Hunter has deployed KHIRA token on BSC(Binance Smart Chain).
The token can be earned while playing our games. It is also a reward provided for
our NFT buyers and users, creating an active ecosystem within the world of Creature Hunter.

Element 01.

Users hunt creatures through mechanics within the Creature Hunters game.
This is implemented using location-based maps and augmented reality (AR) technology.
KHIRA token will be used by our players and NFT users, as everyone can buy NFT with KHIRA or
use KHIRA to purchase in-game items. Users can be rewarded with KHIRA when certain actions are taken in-game.
Creature Hunters will also open on & off line events to further reward our users with KHIRA!

  • KHIRA users will be able to play P2E game, mining KHIRA's native tokens.
  • Users will earn points while playing various P2E platform.
  • Points earned will be able to swap between KHIRA token.
  • Tokens will be safely stored in user's in-game wallet.
  • With the tokens earned, users can choose to either trade, or purchase NFT.
  • NFT purchased will have certain properties which will boost the game experience and contribute to earning more tokens.

Element 02.

NFT market
implemented in the game.

Users purchase NFTs to use better mechanics to hunt creatures.
The purchased NFTs may be upgraded. Of course, it is possible to sell
mechanicals (NFTs) purchased in the NFT market.

Element 03.
